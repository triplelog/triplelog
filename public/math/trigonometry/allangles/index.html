Absolute Value of Sine

  • Reduce theta so that \gcd(a,b)=1 where \theta = \frac{a\pi}{b}.
  • What is the denominator?
  • What is \sin(\frac{\pi}{b})?
  • If b=1,2,3,4, or 6 and a is integer with gcd(a,b)=1 then \sin(\frac{a\pi}{b})=\pm \sin(\frac{\pi}{b}).

Positive or Negative?

  • For cosine, is \frac{a\pi}{b} on left or right?
  • For sine, is \frac{a\pi}{b} on top or bottom?
  • Add or subtract \frac{2b\pi}{b} until -b\lt a\lt b.
  • If a\gt 0 then \sin(\frac{a\pi}{b}) is positive.
  • If |2a|\lt b then \cos(\frac{a\pi}{b}) is positive.
